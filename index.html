How Cyberpunk Works

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world action RPG set in a dystopian future,
where players assume the role of V, a mercenary navigating a sprawling,
high-tech city. The game blends immersive storytelling,
advanced character customization, and impactful choices,
offering players a deeply personal and dynamic gameplay experience.
